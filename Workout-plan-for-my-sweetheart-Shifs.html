Workout (50 minutes)

Circuit 1: Lower Body & Glutes Focus (20 minutes)

Perform each exercise for 45 seconds with 15 seconds of rest between exercises. Complete 3 rounds with 1 minute of rest between rounds.

  • Squats: Keep feet shoulder-width apart, sit back like you're sitting in a chair, and make sure your knees don‚Äôt go beyond your toes. Focus: Quads, hamstrings, and glutes. (Watch Demo)
  • Glute Bridges: Lie on your back with knees bent and feet flat. Raise your hips, squeeze the glutes at the top, and lower slowly. Focus: Glutes and hamstrings. (Watch Demo)
  • Bulgarian Split Squats (Bodyweight): Stand a couple of feet away from a bench or chair, place one foot on the chair behind you, and squat down on the front leg. Focus: Quads, hamstrings, and glutes. (Watch Demo)
  • Lateral Leg Raises: Lie on your side, keeping legs straight. Lift the top leg up and slowly lower it back down. Switch sides halfway through. Focus: Outer thighs and glutes. (Watch Demo)
  • Wall Sit: Press your back against a wall and lower your body into a squat position, holding as long as you can. Focus: Quads, hamstrings, and endurance. (Watch Demo)

Circuit 2: Core & Waist Focus (20 minutes)

Perform each exercise for 40 seconds, followed by 20 seconds of rest. Complete 3 rounds with 1 minute of rest between rounds.

  • Plank (Standard): Hold a plank position with elbows under shoulders, body in a straight line. Focus on keeping your core tight. Focus: Abs and core stabilization. (Watch Demo)
  • Bicycle Crunches: Lie on your back with hands behind your head. Bring one knee toward your chest while rotating your torso to bring the opposite elbow toward that knee. Switch sides. Focus: Obliques and abs. (Watch Demo)
  • Leg Raises: Lie flat on your back with legs straight. Raise your legs towards the ceiling, keeping them straight, and lower slowly. Focus: Lower abs. (Watch Demo)
  • Russian Twists (Bodyweight): Sit with knees bent and feet slightly off the ground (or keep them on the floor if it‚Äôs too hard). Twist your torso from side to side, tapping the floor next to your hip. Focus: Obliques and waist. (Watch Demo)
  • Side Plank (Each Side): Lie on your side, propped up on your elbow. Lift your hips to form a straight line with your body. Hold for 20 seconds on each side. Focus: Obliques and core. (Watch Demo)

Circuit 3: Upper Body & Chest (10 minutes)

Perform each exercise for 45 seconds, followed by 15 seconds of rest. Complete 2 rounds.

  • Push-Ups: If regular push-ups are too difficult, modify by doing knee push-ups. Focus: Chest, shoulders, and triceps. (Watch Demo)
  • Tricep Dips (Using a Chair or Bench): Place hands on the edge of a chair or bench, lower your body, then push back up. Keep elbows close to your body. Focus: Triceps. (Watch Demo)
  • Incline Push-Ups (Feet Elevated): Place your feet on a raised surface like a chair or couch. This will target the upper part of your chest more. Focus: Upper chest and shoulders. (Watch Demo)
  • Superman Hold: Lie face down on the floor and raise your arms and legs off the ground, holding this position for 10-20 seconds. Focus: Lower back and glutes (helps with posture and balance). (Watch Demo)
